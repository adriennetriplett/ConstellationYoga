About Classes

At Constellation Yoga, we offer classes focusing on six different styles of yoga. Based on your experience level and your goals, we encourage you to select the yoga practice that feels most appropriate for your needs.

HATHA

A gentle introduction to the most basic yoga postures. You will leave class feeling longer, looser, and more relaxed.

ASHTANGA

Ashtanga, based on ancient yoga teachings, is a rigorous style of yoga that follows a specific sequence of postures and links every movement to a breath. This is a sweaty, physically demanding practice.

VINYASA

Vinyasa consists of fluid, movement-intensive practices, smoothly transitioning from pose to pose, with the intention of linking breath to movement. If you hate routine and love to test your physical limits, vinyasa may be for you.

BIKRAM

In our Bikram class, you will sweat like never before as you work your way through a series of 26 poses in a room heated to 95 degrees. Due to the heated conditions of the studio, don't forget to bring a water bottle!

YIN

Yin yoga is a delicious way to relax and soothe frayed nerves. This restorative class uses holsters, blankets, and blocks to prop students into passive poses so the body can experience the benefits of a pose without having to exert any effort.

If you're feeling uncertain about which practice is right for you, feel free to speak with one of our instructors, who will be happy to guide you down the right path.
